About

I am an engaging and highly motivated PhD student specialising in Machine Learning and enrolled under CDT in Mathematical Modelling, Analysis, and Computation (MAC-MIGS CDT), jointly supervised by Desmond Higham and Francesco Tudisco at University of Edinburgh.

My current research interests span a diverse range of topics including discrete diffusion language models, agentic LLMs, graph neural networks, and physics-inspired representation learning enhanced with oscillatory dynamics.

Prior to this, I worked on adversarial attacks targeting image classifiers, LLMs, and machine-unlearned diffusion models. I also gained experience with optical neural networks during my Bachelor's degree.
